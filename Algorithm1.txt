Algorithm 1: Connecting Pairs of Persons

Requirement:

Python3

How to execute:

1. Open terminal in folder with Algorithm1.py

2. Run program with "python3 algorithm1.py"

3. Ensure seating numbers have spaces (Ex. if couples = 6, 4 10 11 5 7 3 9 2 1 0 6 8)

The program will then output the total number of swaps it took
